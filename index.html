Who We Are

Founded in 2023 and based in Amroha, Uttar Pradesh, Bright Career Placements is a specialized training and placement consultancy dedicated to empowering job seekers and supporting employers in their hiring needs.

We focus on interview preparation, skill alignment, and comprehensive recruitment support to ensure candidates are not just placed, but placed successfully with the confidence and skills to thrive in their roles.
